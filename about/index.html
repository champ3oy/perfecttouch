About Us
PERFECT TOUCH HOME NURSING is a home care agency established to meet the physical, emotional, social and medical needs of the aged, chronically ill, the disabled, lactating mothers and individuals who are discharged from the hospital and need an assistance in the comfort of their homes. We provide families with licensed and certified nurses to assist our clients in their own homes with activities of daily living at an affordable cost. Our vision is to relieve busy families and lactating mothers of the stress of having to deal with their aged ,chronically ill, disabled and new-borns.
